About the hub

Built for how your team actually works.

Design requests used to travel through Microsoft Forms, land in an Outlook inbox, get manually moved into Monday.com, and then require individual follow-ups to track. Every step was manual overhead.

Aurigo Design Hub closes that gap. Requests come in, tasks get assigned by bandwidth and expertise, statuses update, and nothing falls through.

01

Intake without the admin

Tasks created from requests, auto-named by format, assigned by expertise and current workload.

02

Approval chain, tracked

Design → Snigdha review → stakeholder sign-off → print or publish. Every step accounted for.

03

End-of-day reports, automated

Status summaries generated daily. No status-check messages. No chasing updates.

04

Team load at a glance

See who's overloaded and who's the right fit before you assign anything.
